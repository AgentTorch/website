Large Population Models

realistic. secure. reliable agent-based intelligence

Self-organized complexity emerges everywhere from cells to societies. Large population models reveal this collective intelligence by bridging mechanistic simplicity with behavioral richness. LPMs help model the morphogenesis of organs by simulating cell interactions, understand pandemics by tracking disease transmission between individuals, and study online dynamics by recreating interactions between avatars. By pioneering techniques to securely access populations, efficiently scale simulations and enable reliable analysis, LPMs establish a practical toolkit for tackling challenges inside the body, around us and beyond. With rigorous calibration, validation and transparency, large population models provide accessible digital twins of emergent phenomena - making complexity simple again.
