LPMs in action

LPMs in action

Scalability

AgentTorch can simulate country-size populations in seconds on commodity hardware.

Differentiability

AgentTorch models can differentiate through simulations with stochastic dynamics and conditional interventions, enabling gradient-based learning.

Composition with DNNs

AgentTorch models can compose with deep neural networks (DNNs) to describe autonomous agent behavior, calibrate simulation parameters to multi-modal data and specify expressive interaction rules.

Generalization

AgentTorch helps simulate diverse ecosystems - humans in geospatial worlds, cells in anatomical worlds, autonomous avatars in digital worlds.

AgentTorch is building the future of decision engines - inside the body, around us and beyond!
